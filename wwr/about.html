History

Since 1990, White Water Rafting Adventures has been leading thrilling adventures on some of the most exciting rivers in the world. Our experienced guides ensure a safe and memorable experience for all our guests.

We started as a small family-owned business and have grown into one of the most trusted names in the rafting industry. Join us and be part of our story!
